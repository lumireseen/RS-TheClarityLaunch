Why the Pasture Trust Matters

Native grasslands are among the world's most threatened ecosystems, yet they are crucial for carbon sequestration, water retention, and supporting endangered species. The Pasture Trust acquires and manages these vital lands, ensuring they are protected from development forever.

Your support doesn't just save land; it empowers sustainable grazing practices, funds educational programs for farmers, and provides necessary resources for essential habitat restoration. Every dollar directly supports the soil, the wildlife, and the future of our planet.
